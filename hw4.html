The best website/platform for “Competitive programming”

I suggest “Exercism” as the best website/platform for “Competitive programming”. There are 3,173 exercises in 51 languages to enhance your programming skills. It starts off with language tracks that allow users to choose their preferred languages. We can choose the language that we are good at. Moreover, there are human mentors who will check your code and help you improve as you progress. This makes the platform perfect for total beginners who want to deepen their understanding of a new programming language.
